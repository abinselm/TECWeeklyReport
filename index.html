IT Operations

  • The laptops security compliance issue has been fully resolved for all users who were affected in Aden office and the Hubs. The IT Support Team remains on standby and ready to assist any newly affected staff should the issue occur.
  • As part of the WH Network Phase II project, the replacement microwave link has been successfully installed on the tower of building A to strengthen and provide more stable internet connectivity for the warehouses and currently being monitored.
  • The IT team has shared with Admin Engineering the estimated Bill of Quantities (BoQ) for IT works in the additional floor of the new Guest House building.
  • IT team has reinstalled and configured a new HP Color Printer M571 in Building A.
  • IT team has installed and configured an HP Color Printer M570 in Building B. Both devices are now connected to the network and available for staff use.
  • IT team has provisioned and deployed replacement Windows workstations for some staff including Susan, Nino, and Sally Mohammed using Microsoft Autopilot. The devices were enrolled in Intune, and details were updated in GEMS.
  • Conducted a site visit to Al-Nahdi2 to inspect the fiber optic cable pathway and manholes following ongoing road repair work.
  • Checked, programmed, and activated IT Team access cards for the new IT storage location containers located at Al-Gwizi Warehouse.
  • Coordinated with the service provider (Teleyemen) to diagnose and resolve a service outage affecting the Ku-Band VSAT at the WFP Aden main office.
  • Liaised with the Sharp photocopier technician to schedule comprehensive maintenance and servicing for all Sharp photocopiers in the main WFP office and warehouses.
  • A new security compliance restriction has been implemented by HQ for all users in Yemen, impacting several laptops across the Aden office and hubs. Three staff members in Mokha, including one currently on a mission in Turbah, were affected. A solution has been provided to all impacted staff.
  • Following coordination with Aden TEC team, two laptops have been prepared in Aden for Turbah staff affected by the compliance restrictions. They are ready for immediate dispatch.
  • The IT in Mukalla Hub is repairing the CCTV system.
  • WAN optimization in Mukalla hub is being performed to ensure proper load balancing.
  • Systems and networks in Mukalla Hub are being actively monitored to maintain service availability.
  • The IT in Mukalla Hub is coordinating with the Aden IT Team regarding the HQ compliance issue affecting four laptops in the hub.
  • The Mukalla IT has supported affected staff by enabling the use of VDI as a temporary workaround.
  • The Mukalla IT has followed up with the Aden IT Team to arrange for two replacement laptops, which have been sent and received.
