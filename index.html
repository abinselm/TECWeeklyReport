        From 30.Aug - 05.Sep, 2025


IT Operations

  • A new laptops security compliance restriction has been applied by HQ for all Yemen users impacting many users’ laptops in Aden office and Hubs, IT team doing their best providing the support to the affected users facing Outlook and Teams access issues.
  • IT team has completed and submitted the technical analysis for FortiGate tender (Aden AO) to the Procurement Unit.
  • The official letter to MOPIC was prepared and submitted by the IT team, including the list of telecom towers used in the south for WFP operations.
  • Two new casual laborers joined the IT team this week to assist with PCAR 2025 preparations and the asset relocation exercise.
  • As part of ongoing support to warehouse operations, the IT team installed a new point-to-point microwave link under Warehouse Network Upgrade Phase II to further optimize connectivity and minimize interruptions.
  • The IT team made significant progress in diagnosing and resolving a corporate-wide Microsoft 365 access issue affecting multiple users and platforms after applying the new laptop compliance policy.
  • Coordinated the installation of a dedicated stabilizer and UPS at the Al-Nahdah2 site to support the new Xerox B8045 photocopier, ensuring stable power and device protection.
  • Conducted daily checks of network infrastructure and connectivity at the Turba UN Hub and main office to ensure optimal performance.
  • The IT team performed comprehensive hardware and software maintenance on the Xerox photocopier in Building B.
  • Performed daily on-site service checks of IT network infrastructure, connectivity, and peripherals at the main WFP office.
  • Provided technical support for OMBUDS sessions in the Aden office by preparing conference setups and verifying the functionality of audio/visual systems for the Management, Logistics, and Programme units.
  • Conducted a site visit to Al-Nahdah2 to check the fibre cable infrastructure, and another to Algwizi WH to verify the access control system installed for the new IT storage containers.
  • A new Satphone dock station with an external antenna was installed in the Safe Room at the Al-Mukalla hub to ensure robust emergency communications.
  • Upgraded the Security Assistant's laptop in the Mukalla hub to Windows 11.
  • Installed a new IP-PBX desk phone in the Mukalla hub meeting room to improve communication capabilities.
  • Provided daily technical assistance in the Mukalla hub, including printer/scanner configuration (e.g., Scan-to-Email), conference room A/V setup for various units, and resolution of specific Microsoft 365 access issues for three departmental laptops.
  • Dismantled the fingerprint device from the old guest house in Mukalla as part of the ongoing equipment decommissioning process.
  • Implemented WAN optimization and load balancing configurations for the Mukalla hub network to enhance performance and reliability.

ETC

  • Ongoing Assessment for the feasibility of extending ETC connectivity to support OCHA office in Aden.
  • Providing WFP staff VHF handheld radios using call signs as per UN standards.
  • Successfully maintained and ensured continuity of ETC connectivity services for UN hubs in remote locations.
