IT Operations

  • A new photocopier has been deployed in Alnahdi Warehouse to support operations, with coordination ongoing with Admin Maintenance for installation of power protection devices.
  • Asset reorganization and updates in GEMS at Algwizi Warehouse IT storage containers are ongoing as part of preparations for PCAR 2025.
  • The total tickets that were closed this week are 105 tickets while 9 are pending.
  • Performed scheduled maintenance on the CCTV system for network server rooms to ensure continuous security and operation.
  • Reorganized and streamlined user accounts in Active Directory by moving them into their correct security groups, improving access management.
  • Resolved a WiFi connectivity issue at the Almocha Guest House, restoring full service.
  • Monitored systems and network performance in the Mukalla hub office to ensure high availability and proactively address potential issues.
  • Reviewed and approved the consolidated warehouse assessment report from TEC side, providing connectivity recommendations.
  • Scheduled periodic maintenance for all Xerox and Sharp photocopiers in coordination with certified technicians.
  • Updated the address books on three photocopiers to reflect the latest staff contact list.
  • Initiated configuration of "Scan to Email" services on the backup HP 527 printer via the WFP Amazon platform in Mukalla hub.
  • Ongoing coordination with Smart Net technician for the installation of a new fingerprint access system in the Algwizi IT storage room.
  • Continued the verification and update of IT assets and inventory in GEMS as preparation for the PCAR in Mocha and Mukalla offices.
